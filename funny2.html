VACATION ACTIVITIES

Traveling and doing fun activities is part of any vacation!

During vacations people often like to travel to exotic and new places, and maybe try something new that they've never done before! This is fun and all, but are there things that sound like fun while they actually are quite horrifying? I have compiled a small list of things that might sound fun if you've never heard of them, but you definitely should not try.

Here are my top three activites that sound fun if you haven't heard of them, but really aren't.
